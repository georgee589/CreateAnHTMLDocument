Pokémon Red & Blue for GAME BOY

WHAT ARE POKéMON?

Pokémon are found throughout the world and come in many shapes and sizes.
Some people have them as pets, others use them for battling and research.

YOUR ADVENTURE BEGINS

You are a young, aspiring trainer whose dream is to become the absolute best like no one ever was.
Along the way, you must battle aganist rival trainers, and catch as many Pokémon as you can!
